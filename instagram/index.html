The Team

Kevin Systrom (CEO, co-founder)

Kevin Synstrom (@kevin) is the CEO and co-found of instagram, a community of more than 1 billion who captured and shared the world's moments on the service. He is responsible for the company's overall vision and strategy as well as day-to-day.

Since the beginning, Kevin has focused on simplicity and inspiring creativity through solving problems with thoughtful product design. As a result, instagram has become the home for visual storytelling for everyone from celebrities, newsrooms and brands, to teens, musicians and anyone with creativity passion.

Prior to founding instagram, Kevin was part of the startup Odeo, which later became Twitter, and spent two years at Google working on a products like Gmail and Google Reader. He graduated from Standford University with a BS in management Science & Engineering and serves on the boards of Walmart and KCRW.
